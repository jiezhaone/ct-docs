# Logging Practice

## How to Log

1. On the Home screen, tap the practice activity you want to log
2. Enter today's count
3. If the activity has subcategories (e.g., service type, meditation type), pick the category first
4. Tap "Submit" to complete the entry

## Rules

- Each activity can be logged once or multiple times per day
- Submitted records appear in your History
- Records must be stamped by a master before counting toward passport progress

::: warning
Submitted records can be deleted, but stamped records cannot.
:::

## Editing a Record

Only the **quantity** can be edited. The date and subcategory cannot be changed.

1. From the Home screen, tap the activity to open its detail page
2. In this month's records, find the entry to edit and tap the quantity (it has a pencil icon)
3. The edit panel pops up from the bottom — use "+" / "−" to adjust the quantity
4. Tap "Confirm" to save

::: warning
Stamped records (those with a green "Stamped" tag) cannot be edited — the quantity is not tappable — and cannot be deleted.
:::

## Deleting a Record

1. From the Home screen, tap the activity to open its detail page
2. The bottom of the page lists this month's records with the hint "Swipe left to delete"
3. **Swipe left** on the record you want to remove — a red delete icon appears on the right
4. Tap the delete icon and confirm in the dialog

::: warning
- Stamped records (with the green "Stamped" tag) cannot be swiped to delete
- Deletion cannot be undone — confirm before proceeding
:::

## Backdating

If you forgot to log on a previous day, you can backdate a record:

1. On the log screen, tap the date field
2. Choose the date you want to backdate
3. Enter that day's count
4. Tap "Submit" to complete

## Meritorious Service — Note

When logging Meritorious Service, switching the service type automatically resets the quantity to that type's default:
- Center service: defaults to 1 hour
- Mountain-retreat service: defaults to 1 time
- Continuous service: defaults to 3 days
