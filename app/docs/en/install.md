# Download & Install

## Android

1. Go to the [Google Play Store](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=org.ctpassport.app)
2. Search for "CT Passport"
3. Tap "Install"

Or scan the QR code below with your phone camera to open Google Play directly:

<img src="/qr-android.png" alt="Google Play QR Code" width="220" />

## iOS

1. Go to the [App Store](https://apps.apple.com/app/%E7%A6%8F%E6%85%A7%E5%AD%B8%E7%BF%92%E8%AD%B7%E7%85%A7/id6760577998)
2. Search for "CT Passport"
3. Tap "Get"

Or scan the QR code below with your iPhone camera to open the App Store directly:

<img src="/qr-ios.png" alt="iOS App Store QR Code" width="220" />

## Web Version

No installation required — open [app.ct-passport.org](https://app.ct-passport.org) in your browser.

::: tip
Please ensure your device meets the minimum OS requirements: Android 7.0 or above / iOS 13.0 or above. For the web version, Chrome or Safari is recommended.
:::
