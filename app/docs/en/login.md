# Login & Registration

## Login Steps

1. Open the CT Passport app
2. Enter your student number
3. Tap "Verify"
4. Confirm that the displayed personal information is correct
5. Tap "Confirm Login"

::: tip
Student accounts are created by your center's Dharma master. If you do not yet have an account, please contact your master.
:::

## Quick Login

The app remembers the most recently used student numbers (up to 5). On the next login, a "Recent Logins" list appears below the input field — tap any entry to fill the number automatically.

::: tip
Quick login entries are stored locally on the device. They will be cleared if you switch devices or clear the app's data.
:::

## Web Version

CT Passport is also available on the web at [app.ct-passport.org](https://app.ct-passport.org). It works the same way as the app.
