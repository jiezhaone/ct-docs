# Notifications

Announcements and notifications from your center or admins are available on the Notifications page.

## Opening Notifications

Tap the **bell icon** in the top-right corner of the Home screen. Unread notifications show a red badge with the unread count.

## Viewing Notifications

Notifications are sorted by time, newest first. Each entry shows:
- **Title** and **summary** (up to two lines)
- **Sent time** (e.g., "5m ago", "2d ago", or an absolute date)
- **Unread marker**: a gold dot on the left side of unread items

Tap a notification to expand and read the full content; it is also marked read automatically. Tap again to collapse.

## Pull to Refresh

Pull down at the top of the list to refresh and fetch the latest notifications.

## Language

Notification content is shown in the matching language for your current app setting (Chinese or English), if the admin provided bilingual content.
