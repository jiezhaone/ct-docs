# Practice Content Management

## Viewing the Sutra List

Under "Sutra Recitation & Group Practice", tap "Recite" or "Manage Sutras" in the detail page to view the catalog of sutras provided by the system. You then pick the ones you are currently practicing.

### Search and Sectioned View

The "My Sutras" page is split into two sections for easier management:

- **Selected (N)**: sutras already in your personal list, sorted by name, shown at the top
- **Available (N)**: sutras not yet selected, in the system's default order

The search bar at the top filters by **sutra name in Chinese or English** in real time. While searching, both sections remain visible — only matching items are shown — so you can quickly find what you need even with a long catalog.

## Submitting a Sutra Record

1. Tap "Log" under "Sutra Recitation & Group Practice"
2. Choose the sutra
3. Enter the quantity
4. Tap "Submit"

## Browsing Dharma Excerpts

Under "Dharma Excerpts", tap "Read" to open the excerpt image viewer:

- **Swipe left / right**: switch to the previous / next excerpt
- **Pinch to zoom**: zoom in to see details (up to 4×); horizontal swiping is temporarily disabled while zoomed
- **Bookmarks**: save frequently-read excerpts for quick access
- **Auto-resume**: opens at the last excerpt you read

### Web Version Extras
- Left / right arrow buttons switch excerpts
- Arrow keys (← →) on the keyboard paginate quickly

## Studying Dharma Lectures

Under "Dharma Lectures", tap "Study" to open lecture content. If a page contains internal links, the back button first navigates back within the page before closing it.

### Managing Your Lecture List

Similar to managing sutras, you can pick the lectures you are studying from the system catalog:

1. Open the "Dharma Lectures" detail page
2. Tap "Manage Lectures"
3. Tick or untick lectures to include in your personal list
4. Selections are saved automatically

When logging a lecture-study record, you only need to enter the quantity and date — no need to specify which lecture per entry.
